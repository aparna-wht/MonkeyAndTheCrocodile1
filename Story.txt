**Story**
Once Upon a time , in a forest, there lived a monkey who resided on a jamun (berry) tree,
Which was on the banks of a river. In the same forest there lived a crocodile and his wife.

One day the crocodile came to the banks of the river and rested under the trees. The kindhearted monkey offerred him some fruits. 
The crocodile came back the next day for more fruits , as he loved them. As days passed by the crocodile and the monkey became good friends.
